PROBLEM STATEMENT
 Hinge is a dating app whose mission statement is “designed to be deleted.” The app is known for having users who are searching for serious relationships.

It has great features to start a conversation, but not enough to keep it going. To foster meaningful connections, ‍a post-match "date" feature is proposed.

ROLE
Research, Ideation, Design, Prototype

Initial Design Question

How can we make the post-match experience more engaging?

Interview And Observation

What are people's attitudes to online dating apps?

RESEARCH GOALS

1. Understand dating apps and how users interact with Hinge.
‍
‍2. Discover users' post-match actions.

3. Understand users' pain points after having a match.

NEEDFINDING INTERVIEW

The numbers offer us the general dynamic of dating apps including Hinge. To get a more qualitative understanding of the user experience, a needfinding interview was conducted.


Interview tasks:
1. Understanding what features matter when users dating online.
2. Finding out what they liked and disliked about the existing online dating experience.

Participants:
6 participants between the ages of 25 and 33, with a 50:50 male to female ratio.
